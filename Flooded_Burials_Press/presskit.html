Description

Flooded Burials is a top-down action adventure game about exploring a tower buried in the sea. The game consists of an overworld where you can explore and get to know NPCs, and individual stages where you you'll have to face combat and platforming challenges.

Features

  • A beautiful world for you to explore and uncover
  • A deep combat system with unique parrying mechanics
  • Unique npc's you can talk to and get to know.
  • Multiple stages that can be replayed for higher ranks
